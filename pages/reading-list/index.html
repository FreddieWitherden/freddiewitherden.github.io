I love books—specifically those of the non-fiction persuasion—and usually dedicate a few hours each day indulging myself in them. What follows are a series of short reviews of books I have read recently. Given that my interests lie primarily in the fields of physics, applied mathematics and computer science the scope of the list is somewhat limited.

For the benefit of search engines and the bandwidth impaired all reviews are given their own pages. Books are listed in the order they were read in and reviews may be amended over time.
